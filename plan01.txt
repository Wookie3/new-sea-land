# Open Water Boat Game - Development Plan

## Vision
Open world boat game set in the 1900s with semi-realistic WWII/WWI factions. Players freely explore the ocean, battle enemy boats and island bases, while managing fuel and staying alive.

## Phase 1: Core Combat & Fuel (High Priority)

### 1. Fuel System
- Fuel gauge in HUD (alongside health)
- Fuel drains while moving
- Game over if fuel reaches 0
- Fuel pickup stations on islands or floating barrels

### 2. Combat System
- Mouse-aimed cannons (turret)
- Projectile physics
- Reload mechanics
- Visual recoil/camera shake on shoot

### 3. Enemy Island Base
- Shootable structure with health bar
- Returns fire at player
- Spawns in Island Group 2 (enemy territory)

## Phase 2: World Building (High Priority)

### 4. Two Island Groups
- Group 1: Player starting area (friendly islands with fuel)
- Group 2: Enemy territory with the base to destroy
- Distance/compass HUD showing direction to objectives

### 5. Wake Trail Effect
- Visual feedback behind boat
- Foam particle system

### 6. Explosion/Hit Effects
- Particle explosions on impact
- Screen shake when taking damage

## Phase 3: Polish & Atmosphere (Medium Priority)

### 7. Day/Night Cycle
- Dynamic sun/moon lighting
- WWII atmosphere

### 8. Ammunition System
- Limited ammo, pickups on islands
- Reload UI indicator

### 9. Enemy Patrol Boats
- Mobile enemies roaming between islands
- AI that chases player or patrols routes

### 10. Audio
- Engine sounds
- Cannon fire
- Explosions

## Phase 4: Game Flow (Lower Priority)

### 11. Mission System
- "Destroy the Enemy Base" objective tracker

### 12. Save System
- Save fuel, health, location

## Current State
- Boat movement with physics (WASD)
- Wave bobbing simulation
- Health/damage system
- Sinking/respawn mechanic
- Basic HUD (health bar)
- Single island scene

## Game Mechanics
- Simple & arcade sailing (keep current WASD movement)
- Hazards: Environmental + Combat (island bases, future enemy boats)
- Start: Player at Island Group 1, objective to destroy enemy base in Island Group 2
- No NPCs initially, just enemy island base to engage
